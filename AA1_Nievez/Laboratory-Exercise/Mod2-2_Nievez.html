Doctor Strange

Doctor Strange is all about magic and fantasy. It is a marvel movie about a successful and talented but arrogant doctor, he was incredible with his work, but then a tradgedy struck. He was caught in a car accident, thus making his hands disabled for a long time. So he wanted to seek help from a strange doctor. After that he began his journey into finding this doctor, and when he was already about to give up, he found a place that led him to learn about certain magic and martial arts.
It is a wonderful and thrilling movie, it kept me on the edge on my seat the whole time, and the ending was shocking, thus for me it is one of my favorite films, till today.
